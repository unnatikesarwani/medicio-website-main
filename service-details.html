Personalized Care for Every Individual

At Raahat Therapy Centre, every therapy is designed to address unique needs — whether it’s motor skills, communication, learning, or emotional well-being.

Occupational Therapy – Building Independence

Our Occupational Therapy program focuses on enhancing everyday skills and independence. We use a strength-based approach to help children and adults thrive in their daily routines.

  • Improves fine and gross motor skills
  • Enhances sensory integration and focus
  • Encourages independence in self-care and learning

Speech and Language Therapy – Finding Your Voice

Supports children and adults in developing communication and language skills to express themselves confidently.

  • Improves speech clarity
  • Strengthens expressive and receptive language
  • Builds confidence in communication

Physiotherapy – Strength and Mobility

Helps individuals improve movement, balance, and coordination through personalized exercise plans and therapies.

  • Enhances posture and flexibility
  • Restores muscle strength
  • Improves mobility and motor planning

Special Education – Learning Without Limits

Customized educational support designed to help children with learning difficulties achieve their full potential.

  • Individualized Education Plans (IEP)
  • Boosts academic and social skills
  • Encourages inclusive learning

CBT & ABA Therapy – Mind and Behavior

Behavioral and psychological interventions that promote positive growth and emotional well-being.

  • Develops coping strategies
  • Reduces negative behaviors
  • Encourages emotional regulation

Art Therapy – Healing Through Creativity

Uses creative expression and art-based activities to improve mental, emotional, and social health.

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Enhances self-expression
  • Boosts self-esteem and emotional growth
