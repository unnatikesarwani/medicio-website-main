Our Services

Occupational Therapy

Helping children and adults achieve independence in daily living.

  • Sensory Integration Therapy.
  • Fine & Gross Motor Skills Development.
  • Handwriting & School Readiness.
  • Feeding, Dressing & Grooming Skills Training.

Speech & Language Therapy

Building communication and swallowing skills for better quality of life.

  • Speech & Language Delays.
  • Autism Communication Support.
  • Fluency & Stuttering Management.
  • Feeding & Swallowing Therapy.

PhysioTherapy

Restoring strength, balance, and mobility across all age groups.

  • Neuro Physiotherapy (Stroke, CP, Parkinson’s, TBI).
  • Pediatric Physiotherapy (developmental delays, torticollis, toe walking).
  • Orthopedic Rehabilitation (fractures, congenital disorders, post-surgical rehab).
  • Pain Management & Postural Correction.

Special Education

Individualized programs to support academic growth and learning success..

  • Individualized Education Plans (IEPs).
  • Remedial Teaching for Dyslexia, Dysgraphia, Dyscalculia.
  • Academic Coaching & School Readiness.
  • Inclusive Education Support.

Behavioral & Emotional Well-Being

1. Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Evidence-based therapy for emotional resilience and positive behavior.

  • Child & Adolescent CBT (anxiety, ADHD, exam stress).
  • Adult CBT (depression, mood regulation, post-stroke adjustment).
  • Parent Support & Counseling.
2. Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) Therapy

Structured, evidence-based programs for children with autism and behavioral needs.

  • Early Intervention ABA Programs.
  • Social Skills Training.
  • Parent Training in ABA Strategies.
  • Behavior Management Plans.

Art Therapy

Harnessing creativity to heal, express, and grow.

  • Emotional Expression & Stress Relief.
  • Creative Art Sessions for Children with Special Needs.
  • Trauma & Behavioral Support.
